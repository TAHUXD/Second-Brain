# 📝 Notes

Permanent notes on topics, concepts, and things you've learned. These are meant to last and grow over time.

> [!tip] One idea per note
> Keep notes focused on a single concept. Use [[wikilinks]] to connect related ideas instead of cramming everything into one note.

## Good notes to have here

- Concepts you want to remember
- Summaries of books or courses
- How-to references you revisit
- Frameworks and mental models
